About me

I am a graduate student in the Sonnenburg Lab in the Department of Microbiology and Immunology at Stanford University. Previously, I worked at Caribou Biosciences in Berkeley, CA.

Research Interests

The human gut microbiome; metagenomics; metabolomics; host-commensal interaction; diet & nutrition; metabolism; CRISPR biology; gene editing applications
