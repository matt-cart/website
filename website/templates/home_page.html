About me

I am a graduate student in the Sonnenburg Lab in the Department of Microbiology and Immunology at Stanford University. Previously, I was a Research Associate at Caribou Biosciences in Berkeley, CA.

Research Interests

CRISPR biology; gene editing applications; microbial ecology; the human gut microbiome; host-commensal interaction; bioinformatics software and web development
